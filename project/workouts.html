Upper Body Workouts

Push-Ups

Instructions: Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. Pause for a second, then push yourself back up.

Pull-Ups

Instructions: Grab a pull-up bar with an overhand grip. Hang at arm's length, then pull your body up until your chin is above the bar. Pause, then lower yourself back down to the starting position.

Dumbbell Bicep Curls

Instructions: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a pair of dumbbells at arm's length. Curl the dumbbells up to your shoulders, then lower them back down to the starting position.

Lower Body Workouts

Squats

Instructions: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing slightly outward. Lower your body as if you were sitting back into a chair, keeping your back straight. Pause, then push yourself back up to the starting position.

Lunges

Instructions: Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Take a big step forward with your right foot and lower your body until your right knee is bent at a 90-degree angle. Pause, then push yourself back up to the starting position. Repeat on the other side.

Deadlifts

Instructions: Stand with your feet hip-width apart and a barbell in front of you. Bend your knees and grasp the bar with an overhand grip. Keeping your back straight, stand up with the bar, then lower it back down to the starting position.
